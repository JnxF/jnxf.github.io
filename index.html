Francisco Martínez Lasaca

Industrial PhD Student

I am a PhD student at the Autonomous University of Madrid, Spain. I pursue my doctoral studies in colaboration with the company UGROUND, and within the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Lowcomote project.

I hold a MSc in Computer Science from the IT University of Copenhagen, Denmark. Earlier, I received a BSc in Computer Science from the Polytechnic University of Catalonia, Spain.

My primary research interests revolve around Model-Driven Software Engineering. Specifically, on how to lower the entry barrier to domain-specific languages (DSLs), either textual- or graphical-based.
